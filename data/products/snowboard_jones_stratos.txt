Jones Stratos Snowboard

Overview
The Jones Stratos snowboard is a high-performance board designed for advanced and expert riders. It excels in all-mountain conditions, including powder, groomed slopes, and technical terrain.

Shape and Profile
The directional shape improves stability at high speeds, while the hybrid camber profile ensures strong edge hold on icy surfaces and responsiveness during turns.

Construction
The snowboard features a durable wood core with reinforced layers for energy transfer and longevity. This allows riders to maintain control in aggressive carving and challenging descents.

Use Cases
Ideal for freeride enthusiasts, technical riders, and those who enjoy varied snow conditions. Suitable for deep powder, backcountry, and resort runs.

Pricing
The Jones Stratos Snowboard is priced at $699. It is available in multiple sizes ranging from 154cm to 165cm, all at the same price.

Common Questions
The Stratos is not a beginner board and requires experience to handle advanced terrain safely.
